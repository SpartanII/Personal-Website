About Us

ApplyBoard (Easy Education INC) is revolutionizing the way international students apply to universities and colleges in North America. Students from anywhere in the world can easily find and apply to the best matched programs based on their academic background, desired course of study and financial support. ApplyBoard saves more than 90% of students time in searching for schools to apply to. Our mission is to make education abroad possible for students around the world regardless of their nationality, location and education background. We are helping thousands of students to make world a better place. We believe that all students in the world should have the opportunity to study abroad and training in the best schools while also learning another culture and language. ApplyBoard, also helps universities and colleges to find the best international students from all over the world to enrich their campuses with international students from different regions. ApplyBoard also gives educational agents access to the matching algorithm and benefit of using our platform for their students and increase their revenue.
